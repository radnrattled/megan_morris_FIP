About Me

If you ask me to describe myself in one word it would be: Creator

I have a huge passion for creating, whether it be art, words, furniture, atmospheres or relationships, if I can get my hands on it, I’m creating with it. When it comes to my main body of work, my poetry illustrations, my purpose is quite simple: to make you feel something. As an incredibly deep feeling person all I have ever wanted to do is make other people feel things too.
Aside from creating I am also an ex circus performer, dog and horse mom, avid collector of typewriters, vintage jackets, plants and tattoos. I have a huge affinity for the mountains and the ocean and the moon. My favourite thing in the entire world is when someone writes me a handwritten letter or note.
